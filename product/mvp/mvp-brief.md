# MVP Brief

Generated: 2026-03-04

## Problem
- Idea: Client Work OS for freelancers to handle scope, pricing, negotiation, and payment workflows
- Project summary: A focused product system around 4 freelancer business risks (Deal, Scope, Payment, Pricing)
- Primary user pain: Freelancers lose margin and time when client requests are unmanaged and responses are inconsistent

## Target User
- Primary segment: Solo freelancers working with SMB clients (designers, video editors, copywriters)
- Usage context: High-pressure client chats (email/WhatsApp/Slack) where fast, professional responses are required

## Thin-Slice Scope
- In-scope journey 1: Land on `/guides/scope-creep` from search, open Scope tool, generate a revision policy in under 3 minutes
- In-scope journey 2: Copy policy + send a client-facing message template directly from result screen
- In-scope journey 3: See cross-module CTA to Payment module and join waitlist for Deal/Pricing
- Out of scope: Team collaboration, client portal, invoicing system, CRM sync, multilingual generation, analytics dashboard

## Value Hypothesis
- If users can generate and send a clear revision policy during a live client request, then time-to-resolution drops and pricing leakage from extra revisions is reduced.

## MVP Focus Decision

- First module to ship: `Scope` (Scope Guard + Revision Policy Generator)

- Monetization wedge: Paid upgrade for unlimited policy generations and premium wording styles
