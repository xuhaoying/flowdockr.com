# MVP Definition - FlowDockr Scope Guard

## MVP Objective
Ship one sellable workflow that solves scope creep for freelancers in under 30 seconds.

## In Scope
- Scope policy generation page (`/scope`)
- Structured output: revision policy, scope rule, client message, contract clause
- Copy interactions and microfeedback
- Free usage cap and upgrade CTA
- SEO guide funnel into `/scope`

## Out of Scope
- Multi-module orchestration UI
- Team workspace and dashboards
- Advanced analytics suite

## Success Metrics
- Activation: >= 10 users generate at least one policy / first 100 visitors
- Engagement: >= 25% of generators perform at least one copy action
- Quality: <= 10% generation failure rate (excluding user input errors)

## Delivery Plan
- Week 1: core generator + route + landing alignment
- Week 2: 5 SEO guides + CTA optimization
- Week 3: payment protection module scoping

## Launch Gate
- [ ] End-to-end flow completes in <30s on desktop and mobile
- [ ] Outputs are copy-ready without manual cleanup
- [ ] Limit reached state clearly routes to upgrade path
- [ ] Error states are recoverable and understandable
